About

Hello and welcome to Granny's Pies where our goal is your satisfaction. Our fearless leader Mama Rinaldi aka Granny has been baking her famou pies since she was a little girl growing up on the Southern coast of Italy. When she moved to America, she learned all the classics and adapted the recipes to add some of her personal flair. Make sure to try them all! Look below for our locations and details on our most popular pies. Call or email to order for pick-up at one of our two locations.
By the way, if you're still reading this, Granny's most famous recipe is her Lemon Meringue pie. While she is typically pretty quiet about her recipes, she has been more generous lately and has shared her secrets!
