Disrupt Colby

What We Do

We are an entrepreneurial group based out of Colby College that provides resources to help students with their entrepreneurial ventures and visions. We encourage entreprenurial spirits to pursue their dream, vision, and passion by providing helpful resources, mentors, and guidance to help navigate through any problems that might arise.

If you are a dreamer, a visionary, a hustler, or someone who loves to solve everyday problems. Disrupt Colby is the right group for you!
